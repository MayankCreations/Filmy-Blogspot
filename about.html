About Me


Hello and welcome to my corner of the internet!

I'm Mayank Mathur, and this is where I share my journey of visiting the not so visited creations of the Indian Cinema. Whether you're here for engaging stories or just a bit of inspiration, I'm thrilled to have you along for the ride.

I've always been fascinated by Movies and Storytelling. Over the years, I've attended various film festivals and got numerous certificates of Volunteership, which has fuelled my passion for Movies or Documentaries. I started this blog to connect with like-minded individuals, share what I've learned, and hopefully spark a few “aha” moments along the way.

When I'm not exploring cinema, you can find me on LinkedIn browsing various profiles each day looking for potential candidates as I work as an HR Head in one of the Big Four Tech companies Deloitte. I believe in the power of Passion and Discipline, and I strive to infuse that into everything I do.

Indian Cinema is a vibrant and diverse landscape, and I'm excited to share my love for it with you.I believe there's one whole dimension of Indian Cinema that hasn't been explored much due to the exponential rise of commercial cinema during the 1950s. This blog is my passion project, dedicated to exploring the rich tapestry of Indian cinema, from Bollywood blockbusters to regional gems and everything in between.

So join me on this cinematic journey. Grab some popcorn and let's dive into the world of Indian films to explore the stories that have defined generations, celebrate the art of filmmaking, and revel in the cultural phenomena that make Indian cinema truly unique.

Thanks for visiting, and I hope you enjoy what you find here.

Warmly, Mayank Mathur
